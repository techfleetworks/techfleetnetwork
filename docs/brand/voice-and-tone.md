# Tech Fleet — Voice and Tone

Source: Tech Fleet Brand Guide. This file is the canonical reference for every
piece of copy that ships in Tech Fleet Network. All AI/agent edits MUST honor
it. Rules in `mem://style/brand-voice` mirror the highlights for fast recall.

## Three voice traits

| Trait        | Do                                                              | Don't                                                       |
|--------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Welcoming    | Warm greeting, simple words, invite people in                   | Cold corporate language; gatekeeping phrasing               |
| Caring       | Acknowledge effort, offer next step, assume good intent         | Blame the person, mock mistakes, dismiss feelings           |
| Informative  | Plain facts, clear steps, explain *why*                         | Hide info, jargon without a definition, vague status copy   |

## Reading level

Aim for **7th grade**. Run prose through Hemingway-style scoring before merging
copy-heavy changes. CI fails when avg reading-grade > 9 on user-facing strings.

## Tone by surface

| Surface                          | Tone                                       |
|----------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Auth, errors, empty states       | Caring + Informative; never blame          |
| Dashboards, journey, training    | Welcoming + Informative; encouraging       |
| Admin tools                      | Informative + concise; less warmth okay    |
| Transactional emails             | Welcoming + Caring; short, action-led      |
| Legal / policy pages             | Informative; plain language preferred      |

## Praise

Praise behavior, not identity. ✅ "You shipped that feedback round on time."
🚫 "You're a natural leader."

## Humor & emoji

Sparingly. Never at anyone's expense. One emoji per message, max.
